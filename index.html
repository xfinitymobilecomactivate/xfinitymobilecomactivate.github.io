Activate Xfinity Mobile

Xfinitymobile.com/activate is a website that allows users to activate their Xfinity Mobile service. There are four different ways to activate your service: using an eSIM, BYOD, eSIM Quick Transfer, or Old Mobile. No matter which method you choose, activating your Xfinity Mobile service is easy and quick.



Steps to Create an Account on Xfinity Mobile


  1. Visit the Xfinity Mobile website.
  2. Click "Sign In" or "Get Started."
  3. Choose "Create a New Account."
  4. Fill in your information.
  5. Create a username and password.
  6. Verify your identity if required.
  7. Review and accept terms of service.
  8. Complete the account setup.

Activate Your Xfinity Mobile


  1. Visit Xfinitymobile.com/activate.
  2. Sign in or create an account.
  3. Select "Activate Device."
  4. Enter device details.
  5. Follow on-screen instructions.
  6. Restart your phone after activation.

eSIM Quick Transfer


Requirements:

  • Two iPhones with iOS 16 or newer.
  • The cellular plan you want to transfer.

Steps:

  1. Turn on your new iPhone.
  2. Go to Settings > Cellular > Add Cellular Plan.
  3. Choose the plan and tap Transfer.
  4. Enter the verification code if prompted.
  5. Your eSIM is now active, and the old SIM is deactivated.
  6. Follow carrier instructions if prompted.
  7. Optionally, convert a nanoSIM to eSIM in Settings.
  8. Remove the physical SIM from your old iPhone.
